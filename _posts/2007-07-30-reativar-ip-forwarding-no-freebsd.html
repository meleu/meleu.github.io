--- layout: post title: (re)ativar "ip forwarding" no FreeBSD date: '2007-07-30T18:16:00.000-03:00' author: meleu tags: - TCPIP - FreeBSD modified_time: '2007-07-30T18:19:05.174-03:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1613465446497619833.post-4618182146927931268 blogger_orig_url: http://mdicas.blogspot.com/2007/07/reativar-ip-forwarding-no-freebsd.html --- Algumas vezes, depois de usar o ettercap por exemplo, temos que reativar o "IP Forwarding" do kernel. O seguinte comando faz isso:
# sysctl net.inet.ip.forwarding=1
